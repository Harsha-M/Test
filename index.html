Human Resources

If you are a full-time, salaried employee, you are guaranteed paid holidays, given that the holiday falls on a regularly scheduled workday.

Solstice Consulting, LLC generally follows the following holiday schedule:

> New Year’s Day - Tue 1/1/2013
> Memorial Day - Mon 5/27/2013
> Independence Day - Thu 7/4/2013
> Labor Day - Mon 9/2/2013
> Thanksgiving Day - Thur 11/28/2013
> Christmas Day - Wed 12/25/2013

Floating Holidays

You will not be compensated for personal days or floating holidays if you leave the company.
